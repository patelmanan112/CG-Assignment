Article 1 : New Bollywood Movie Released

In September 2025, Bollywood audiences were treated to a diverse array of film releases, spanning genres from action-packed thrillers to heartfelt dramas. Among the notable theatrical releases was Baaghi 4, the latest installment in the action-packed franchise, starring Tiger Shroff and Sanjay Dutt. This film continued the saga of intense combat and personal vendettas, delivering high-octane action sequences that captivated fans.

Another significant release was Jolly LLB 3, the third installment of the popular courtroom comedy series. Featuring Akshay Kumar and Arshad Warsi, the film brought back the witty legal battles and humorous courtroom antics that the franchise is known for, offering a blend of satire and social commentary.

These films, among others released in September 2025, showcased the rich storytelling and diverse cinematic experiences that Bollywood continues to offer, catering to a wide range of audience preferences.

Article 2 : OTT Series Gains Popularity

OTT series have become a dominant force in Indian entertainment in 2025, captivating audiences with diverse genres and compelling narratives. Among the standout series is Criminal Justice: A Family Matter, starring Pankaj Tripathi. This legal thriller has garnered over 27.7 million views, making it the most-watched original OTT show in India this year

Another notable release is Khakee: The Bengal Chapter, a period political thriller created by Neeraj Pandey. Featuring an ensemble cast including Jeet and Prosenjit Chatterjee, the series has been praised for its gripping storyline and strong performances
